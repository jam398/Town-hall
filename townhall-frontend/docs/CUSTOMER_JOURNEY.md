# Customer Journey Maps

This document outlines the key user journeys through the Town Hall Newark website. Each journey maps the user's path from initial awareness to becoming an engaged community member.

---

## Journey 1: First-Time Visitor to Event Attendee

**Persona:** Maria (Curious Beginner)

### Stages

#### 1. Awareness
**Trigger:** Sees Town Hall mentioned on local news, social media, or word of mouth

**Touchpoints:**
- Social media post
- News article
- Friend recommendation
- Google search "AI classes Newark"

**User Thoughts:**
> "What is this? Is it for someone like me?"

**Actions:**
- Clicks link to website
- Lands on homepage

---

#### 2. Discovery
**Goal:** Understand what Town Hall offers

**Touchpoints:**
- Homepage hero section
- Navigation menu
- About page

**User Thoughts:**
> "This looks friendly. Let me see what they do."

**Key Content:**
- Clear value proposition
- "Free and open to everyone" messaging
- Beginner-friendly language

**Actions:**
- Reads hero section
- Scrolls to see events preview
- May visit About page

**Potential Drop-off:**
- Confusing navigation
- Too much jargon
- No clear next step

---

#### 3. Exploration
**Goal:** Find a relevant event

**Touchpoints:**
- Events listing page
- Event detail page
- Blog articles

**User Thoughts:**
> "Is there something for beginners? When is it?"

**Key Content:**
- Clear event tags (Beginner, Workshop)
- Date, time, location prominently displayed
- "No prerequisites" messaging

**Actions:**
- Browses events
- Filters by "Beginner"
- Clicks on interesting event
- Reads event details

**Potential Drop-off:**
- No beginner events available
- Unclear event descriptions
- Inconvenient times/locations

---

#### 4. Decision
**Goal:** Decide to attend

**Touchpoints:**
- Event detail page
- Registration form

**User Thoughts:**
> "This looks good. What do I need to do to sign up?"

**Key Content:**
- "What you'll learn" section
- Instructor bio (builds trust)
- Simple registration form
- Spots remaining indicator

**Actions:**
- Reviews event details
- Checks calendar
- Fills out registration form

**Potential Drop-off:**
- Complex registration process
- Too much information required
- Event appears full

---

#### 5. Registration
**Goal:** Complete signup

**Touchpoints:**
- Registration form
- Confirmation message
- Confirmation email

**User Thoughts:**
> "Did it work? What happens next?"

**Key Content:**
- Simple form (name, email, neighborhood)
- Clear success message
- Confirmation email with details
- Add to calendar option

**Actions:**
- Submits registration
- Receives confirmation
- Adds to calendar

**Potential Drop-off:**
- Form errors
- No confirmation
- Unclear next steps

---

#### 6. Anticipation
**Goal:** Prepare for event

**Touchpoints:**
- Reminder email (1 day before)
- Blog articles
- Discord community

**User Thoughts:**
> "I'm excited but a little nervous. What should I expect?"

**Key Content:**
- Event reminder with logistics
- "What to bring" information
- Related blog articles
- Discord invite for questions

**Actions:**
- Reads reminder email
- May explore blog
- May join Discord

---

#### 7. Attendance
**Goal:** Attend and learn

**Touchpoints:**
- In-person event
- Workshop materials
- Instructor interaction

**User Thoughts:**
> "I hope I can keep up. This is interesting!"

**Key Content:**
- Welcoming environment
- Clear instruction
- Hands-on practice
- Q&A opportunity

**Actions:**
- Attends event
- Participates in activities
- Asks questions
- Meets other attendees

---

#### 8. Follow-up
**Goal:** Continue learning

**Touchpoints:**
- Follow-up email
- Event recording (if available)
- Discord community
- Next event invitation

**User Thoughts:**
> "That was great! What's next?"

**Key Content:**
- Thank you message
- Resources from workshop
- Link to recording
- Upcoming events
- Discord invitation

**Actions:**
- Opens follow-up email
- Watches recording
- Joins Discord
- Registers for next event

---

## Journey 2: Blog Reader to Newsletter Subscriber

**Persona:** James (Concerned Parent)

### Stages

#### 1. Search
**Trigger:** Googles "AI safety for kids" or similar

**Touchpoints:**
- Google search results
- Blog article

**Actions:**
- Finds Town Hall blog article
- Clicks to read

---

#### 2. Read
**Goal:** Get information

**Touchpoints:**
- Blog post
- Related articles

**User Thoughts:**
> "This is helpful and easy to understand."

**Actions:**
- Reads article
- Scrolls to related posts
- May read another article

---

#### 3. Trust
**Goal:** Evaluate source credibility

**Touchpoints:**
- Author bio
- About page
- Other articles

**User Thoughts:**
> "Who writes this? Can I trust them?"

**Actions:**
- Checks author credentials
- Visits About page
- Reads mission statement

---

#### 4. Subscribe
**Goal:** Stay informed

**Touchpoints:**
- Newsletter signup (in article or footer)
- Confirmation

**User Thoughts:**
> "I want to get more content like this."

**Actions:**
- Enters email
- Submits form
- Confirms subscription

---

#### 5. Engage
**Goal:** Become regular reader

**Touchpoints:**
- Newsletter emails
- New blog posts
- Social media

**Actions:**
- Opens newsletters
- Clicks to read new articles
- May share with others
- Eventually attends event

---

## Journey 3: Volunteer Application

**Persona:** Aisha (Career Changer)

### Stages

#### 1. Interest
**Trigger:** Wants to build experience and network

**Touchpoints:**
- Volunteer page
- About page

**Actions:**
- Explores volunteer opportunities
- Reads about roles

---

#### 2. Evaluate
**Goal:** Determine if it's a good fit

**Touchpoints:**
- Volunteer page details
- Team section on About

**User Thoughts:**
> "What would I actually do? Who would I work with?"

**Actions:**
- Reads role descriptions
- Checks time commitment
- Reviews team bios

---

#### 3. Apply
**Goal:** Submit application

**Touchpoints:**
- Volunteer form

**User Thoughts:**
> "I hope they accept me. Let me show my enthusiasm."

**Actions:**
- Fills out form
- Describes skills and interests
- Submits application

---

#### 4. Wait
**Goal:** Receive response

**Touchpoints:**
- Confirmation email
- Follow-up email

**User Thoughts:**
> "Did they get my application? When will I hear back?"

**Actions:**
- Checks email
- May explore more content
- Joins Discord

---

#### 5. Onboard
**Goal:** Start volunteering

**Touchpoints:**
- Welcome email
- Orientation meeting
- Discord volunteer channel

**Actions:**
- Attends orientation
- Joins volunteer Discord
- Starts first assignment

## Journey Optimization Checklist

### For Each Journey Stage:

- [ ] Clear call-to-action
- [ ] Minimal friction
- [ ] Mobile-friendly
- [ ] Accessible
- [ ] Fast loading
- [ ] Error handling
- [ ] Progress indication

### Measurement Points:

| Stage | Metric |
|-------|--------|
| Awareness | Traffic sources, landing page views |
| Discovery | Time on page, scroll depth, bounce rate |
| Exploration | Pages per session, event page views |
| Decision | Registration form starts |
| Registration | Form completion rate |
| Attendance | Event check-in rate |
| Follow-up | Email open rate, Discord joins |
