# User Personas

This document defines the primary user personas for Town Hall Newark. These personas guide design decisions, content strategy, and feature prioritization.

---

## Primary Personas

### 1. Maria - The Curious Beginner

**Demographics:**
- Age: 45-60
- Occupation: Administrative assistant
- Location: Newark, NJ
- Tech comfort: Low to moderate

**Background:**
Maria has heard about AI on the news and from her children. She's curious but intimidated by technology. She uses her smartphone for texting and Facebook but hasn't explored AI tools.

**Goals:**
- Understand what AI is in simple terms
- Learn if AI can help with her daily tasks
- Feel confident talking about AI with family
- Not feel left behind by technology

**Pain Points:**
- Technical jargon is confusing
- Worried about making mistakes
- Doesn't know where to start
- Concerned about privacy and safety

**How Town Hall Helps:**
- Beginner-friendly workshops with no prerequisites
- Content written in plain language
- Supportive community environment
- Hands-on learning with guidance

**Key Pages:**
- Homepage (first impression)
- Events (beginner workshops)
- Blog (AI basics articles)
- About (trust building)

**Quote:**
> "I just want to understand what everyone's talking about without feeling stupid."

### 2. Marcus - The Small Business Owner

- Occupation: Owner of local restaurant/retail shop
- Location: Newark, NJ

- Automate repetitive tasks
- Improve customer communication
- Stay competitive with larger businesses

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time to learn new technology
- Skeptical of hype vs. reality
- Needs solutions that work immediately
- Budget constraints

**How Town Hall Helps:**
- Business-focused workshops

- Practical, actionable content
- Networking with other business owners

**Key Pages:**
- Events (business workshops)
- Blog (AI for business articles)
- Vlogs (quick tips)

**Quote:**
> "Show me something I can use tomorrow to save time or make money."

## Persona Usage Guidelines

### Design Decisions
- Always consider Maria (beginner) when writing content
- Test navigation with less tech-savvy users in mind
- Ensure all features work on mobile (many users are mobile-first)

### Content Strategy
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Avoid jargon or explain it immediately
- Include real Newark examples when possible
- Provide multiple learning formats (text, video, in-person)

### Feature Prioritization
1. Core learning experience (events, blog)
2. Community building (Discord, volunteer)
3. Advanced features (personalization, accounts)

### Accessibility
- All personas may have varying abilities
- Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Test with screen readers
- Provide captions for videos
